Who We Are

Emdadat Tawneya Company was established in 2009 with renewed thinking, desire and ambition to keep pace with the rapid developments in facilities management methods and means. The company develops its modern plans based on the "Saudi Vision 2030" launched by Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman.

The company is always keen to provide its services efficiently, efficiently and professionally, to establish its presence in the Saudi market, through skilled competencies and providing the latest equipment and tools to implement projects, while employing modern technologies to ensure the quality of the executed works.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of professional services designed to meet your needs with efficiency, quality, and care.

Maintenance & Facility Management

We offer comprehensive operation and maintenance services for government and private buildings, ensuring efficient system performance and minimizing downtime. Our team handles routine inspections, repairs, and emergency support, providing reliable solutions tailored to each property's needs for a safe and functional environment year-round.

Specialized Support

We offer comprehensive facilities management support services, along with administrative and technical assistance, to ensure smooth and efficient operations. Additionally, we provide reliable supply services for materials, tools, and spare parts, ensuring your business has everything needed for uninterrupted performance and maintenance.

Air Conditioning, Refrigeration & Heating Systems Maintenance

We provide expert maintenance and operation services for chilled water air conditioning systems (chillers) and concealed air conditioning systems. Our team ensures these systems run efficiently and reliably, maintaining optimal performance and comfort in any environment.

Road Cleaning

We provide comprehensive maintenance, operation, and cleaning services for roads and their associated service facilities. Our team ensures that all infrastructure remains safe, clean, and fully operational, enhancing the overall functionality and durability of these essential services.
